What the metrics mean (plain English)

Points per Game (PPG): average points scored per game (offense). Higher is better.

Yards per Game / per Play: volume and efficiency of movement downfield. Higher is better.

Success Rate: percent of plays that meet down-and-distance goals. Higher is better.

Explosiveness: how big the average successful play is. Higher is better.

PPA (Predicted Points Added): expected points gained per play. Positive = efficient offense; negative = suffocating defense.
